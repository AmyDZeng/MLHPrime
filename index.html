Musec: Play music with just a thought! Using the Muse head-band and Bose SoundTouch system, we allow you to control your sound system by just thinking about it.

How it works:

  • Put on your Muse and open this app.
  • And... Booooom! Your current mood will be translated into music blasted by your Bose.
